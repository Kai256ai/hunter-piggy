# Origin and provenance

HunterPiggy, Cairo Resonance Trajectory Engine, Python Zero, Kai256, Growth and
Shadow Bosons, and Anti-Loop are components of Anna Kai's wider research and
product system.

The Political–Institutional Contingency (PIC) framework was developed by Ian
Tasker. The six-lever framework is used here with his permission. This project
does not claim authorship of PIC. The PIC–Boson mapping, computational adapter,
trajectory implementation, and HunterPiggy integration are separate additions.

HunterPiggy's original scope includes detection and prioritisation of correlated
scam, manipulation, suspicious-link, obfuscation, sensitive-context, and
structural anomaly signals. PIC does not replace that core; it extends the
system with an institutional-mechanism lens.
